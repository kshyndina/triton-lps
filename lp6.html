Audience categories — four creative options

More playful, less corporate. Same 4 categories + logos, four new ways to present them.

Option E · Stacked accordion cards

Four cards laid horizontally. At rest, each shows a small icon + a vertical category title. Click one — it expands wide, others shrink to thin spines. Spotify "stacked cards" / Pinterest "browse" vibe. Always shows all 4 categories at a glance.

Option F · Editorial bento

Asymmetric 4-tile grid. One feature tile (left, full height, gradient bg). Three smaller tiles right, each tinted differently (peach / mint / lilac). Big editorial typography. Logos as inline chips. No interaction needed — it's all visible at once. Magazine-cover energy.
